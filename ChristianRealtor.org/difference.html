Pillar 02

Real Numbers,
Done Personally

A Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) is one of the most important tools in real estate — it's how you know whether a price is fair, competitive, or worth negotiating.

Many agents rely on automated tools or hand this off to assistants. We do it personally, on every property, because understanding your local market deeply is part of doing right by his clients.

You'll sit down with Luke and go through the comparable properties together — so you're not just told a number, you understand why it's the right number.

What Goes Into Your CMA

  • Recently sold homes in your specific neighborhood
  • Active and pending listings you're competing with (or against)
  • Adjustments for size, condition, upgrades, and lot differences
  • Current market trends and how quickly homes are moving
  • A clear, honest conversation about pricing strategy
