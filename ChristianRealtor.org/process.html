How It Works

Buying or Selling on
Florida's Gulf Coast?

Real estate doesn't have to feel overwhelming. Whether you're purchasing your first home or preparing to sell, here's a simple, honest breakdown of each step — so you always know what's next.

The Process

A Clear Path
From Start to Closing

Pick the path that fits you — buying or selling — and follow along step by step. We're with you every step of the way.

1

Get Pre-Approved

Before you start touring homes, connect with a lender to find out what you can comfortably afford. Use our free Mortgage Calculator to estimate your monthly payment. A pre-approval letter shows sellers you're a serious, ready buyer — and helps you shop with confidence.

💡 We recommend: Luis Peguero, Fairway Independent Mortgage — loanswithluis.com
2

Define Your Wish List

We'll sit down with you to talk through your must-haves, nice-to-haves, budget, and preferred neighborhoods — so every home you see is one worth considering.

3

Tour Homes Together

We'll set up showings, walk through each property with you, and offer honest feedback — pointing out both the strengths and potential concerns of each home.

4

Make an Offer

Found "the one"? We'll help you craft a competitive offer based on local market data, guide you through negotiations, and represent your best interests every step of the way.

5

Inspection & Appraisal

Once your offer is accepted, a professional inspection and lender-ordered appraisal help confirm the home's condition and value — giving you peace of mind before moving forward.

💡 We recommend: William Larson, Larson Inspections — larsoninspection.com
6

Closing Day

Sign the final paperwork, transfer funds, and get your keys! We'll walk you through everything so closing day feels exciting — not stressful.

Ready to start your home search?

Get in Touch
1

Initial Consultation & Home Valuation

We'll visit your home, walk through it with you, and provide an honest assessment of its market value based on recent local sales and current conditions.

2

Prepare & Stage Your Home

Together, we'll identify simple updates, repairs, or staging touches that can help your home shine and attract stronger offers — without unnecessary expense.

3

List & Market the Property

We'll create professional listing materials and market your home across the MLS and online platforms to reach as many qualified buyers as possible.

4

Review Offers

As offers come in, We'll walk you through each one — explaining the terms, contingencies, and trade-offs — so you can make a confident, informed decision.

5

Negotiate & Accept

We'll negotiate on your behalf to get the best possible terms, then guide you through accepting an offer and preparing for the steps ahead.

6

Closing Day

Once inspections and paperwork are complete, you'll sign the final documents, hand over the keys, and officially close this chapter — with Luke by your side.
